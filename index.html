About

Hi, I'm Sai Sri Charan Yerramsetty — a Hyderabad-based photographer and visual storyteller.

Through Film Frxmes, I aim to preserve the raw atmosphere and fleeting emotion of a single moment — whether it's the thunderous roar of a McLaren engine, the quiet energy of a city street, or the vibrant chaos of cultural celebrations.

Every frame is a deliberate choice. Every shot, a story.
